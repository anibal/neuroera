Teeth Whitening

Professional teeth whitening is one of the quickest ways to refresh your smile. Using advanced whitening systems, we can safely remove stains caused by coffee, tea, smoking, and aging. The results are instantly visible and can make your smile look brighter and healthier.

Porcelain Veneers

Veneers are thin, custom-made shells that cover the front surface of teeth to correct chips, gaps, discoloration, and uneven shapes. They are crafted to match your natural tooth color, giving you a flawless, long-lasting smile.

Dental Bonding

Bonding involves applying a tooth-colored resin to repair minor chips, cracks, or gaps. It’s a cost-effective and minimally invasive way to enhance your smile, often completed in a single visit.

Gum Contouring

Sometimes, a beautiful smile is about more than just your teeth. Gum contouring reshapes the gum line to create better proportions and symmetry, especially if you have a "gummy smile" or uneven gum levels.

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers

Q: How often should I visit the dentist?

You should visit the dentist for a check-up and cleaning every six months to maintain healthy teeth and prevent future dental problems.

Q: Do you accept insurance or offer payment plans?

Yes! We accept most major UK dental insurance plans, and we also offer flexible payment options to make treatment more affordable.

Q: I’m nervous about dental visits. Can you help?

Yes! We offer a calm, gentle dental experience and can provide comfort options to help you feel relaxed and confident during your visit.

Q: What treatments do you offer?

We provide a wide range of treatments including general dentistry, cosmetic procedures, restorative care, orthodontics, and preventive services to support your long-term oral health.

Q: Are you accepting new patients?

Yes! We’re currently welcoming new patients and would love to help you achieve a healthy, confident smile with personalised dental care.
