This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data when you access our services, website, or mobile applications.

1. Introduction

At, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring your privacy is respected at all times. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, book an appointment, or use any of our services. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms described in this policy. We value your trust and are dedicated to being transparent about how your data is handled, ensuring that you have full control over your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect both personal and non-personal information to provide you with the best possible dental care and service experience. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and any details you share through our contact forms or during appointment bookings. Additionally, we may collect technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and pages you visit on our website for analytics purposes. This information helps us understand how our website is used, improve our services, and ensure a secure browsing experience.

  1. Basic identification details (name, email address, phone number)
  2. Medical history and relevant dental health records
  3. Insurance and payment information for billing purposes
  4. Appointment history and treatment preferences

“Confidentiality isn’t just a policy – it’s our promise.”

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Information may only be shared in limited cases, such as:

  • Coordinating with your insurance provider
  • Referrals to specialist dentists or healthcare providers (with your consent)
  • Compliance with legal obligations or court orders

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for providing exceptional dental services and maintaining smooth communication with you. This may include:

  • Scheduling, confirming, and reminding you about appointments
  • Offering personalized treatment recommendations based on your dental needs
  • Processing payments and managing insurance claims
  • Sending important updates, offers, or educational content related to your oral health

5. Data Security Measures

Your personal information is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access. We use industry-standard encryption and secure systems to ensure your data remains safe

We take the protection of your personal information seriously. Our website uses secure servers, SSL encryption, and other technical measures to prevent unauthorized access, data loss, or misuse. Only authorized personnel with a legitimate reason to access your information are granted permission to do so. While no system can guarantee 100% security, we continuously monitor and update our security protocols to safeguard your data as effectively as possible.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. You may also opt out of marketing communications or withdraw consent for data processing where applicable. If you have any concerns about how your data is handled, please contact us directly, and we will work to address your request promptly and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, technology, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information. Continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
